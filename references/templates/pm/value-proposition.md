# Value Proposition

## Customer jobs

[What functional, social, and emotional jobs is the user trying to accomplish?]

## Pains

[What annoys, risks, or bad outcomes does the user experience before, during, or after trying to get the job done?]

## Gains

[What outcomes or benefits does the user want or expect?]

## Pain relievers

[How does this product eliminate or reduce specific pains?]

## Gain creators

[How does this product create specific gains for the user?]

## Unique value

[What can the user get here that they cannot get anywhere else?]

## Existing alternatives

[What is the current state of the art? What would people use if this did not exist?]

## Why now

[What changed recently that makes this possible or necessary now?]

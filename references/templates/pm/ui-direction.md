## Aesthetic direction

[Pick one or blend two. Each has specific implications for typography, color, and motion.]

- **Brutally Minimal**: Black, white, one accent. Maximum whitespace. No decoration. (Linear, Notion)
- **Editorial/Magazine**: Strong typography hierarchy, generous margins, serif headings. (Medium, The Verge)
- **Retro-Futuristic**: Neon accents, dark backgrounds, monospace elements, terminal aesthetics.
- **Luxury/Refined**: Muted palette, serif or geometric sans, slow motion, premium spacing.
- **Playful/Toy-like**: Rounded shapes, bright palette, bouncy motion, illustration-forward.
- **Brutalist/Raw**: Raw edges, high contrast, unconventional layouts, exposed structure.
- **Organic/Natural**: Earth tones, soft shapes, nature-inspired motion, breathing whitespace.
- **Industrial/Utilitarian**: Monospace, data-dense, function-first, minimal decoration. (Bloomberg, GitHub)
- **Maximalist Chaos**: Rich color, layered elements, dynamic motion, information-dense.
